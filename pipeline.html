Automation Summary
11 of 15 metrics auto-populate once the eight brief fields are complete. The four remaining gates — Cultural Truth (human), Moment Respect (buyer review), Attribution Clarity (measurement instrument field), and the Consult escalation override — are structurally human by design. That is the product's defensibility, not its limitation.
The Attribution Bridge
Brief field 08 (Measurement Instrument) directly controls the Attribution Clarity floor. No instrument → default 0.61 (|ψ|² prior). Add a QR+promo code or digital pairing → floor moves to the 0.72–0.78 band. Full closed-loop lift study → 0.84+. The measurement plan is part of the buy, not an afterthought.
Falsification Loop
The KPI targets declared in Brief field 07 are locked at the time of the verdict and surfaced again at flight close. Every scored outcome is logged against the verdict that produced it. The system accumulates a calibration record: which verdicts predict hits, which buyers' overrides beat the model, which channels close the signal gap.
